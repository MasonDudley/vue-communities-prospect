## Business Overview
- The business markets student housing near Liberty University.
- The site presents two communities:
  - Oasis
  - The VUE at Cornerstone
- The office/contact hub is centered at Oasis.

## Core Leasing Model
- Individual leases are used.
- Residents rent their room only, not the entire apartment.
- Residents are not financially responsible for roommates' portions of rent under the individual-lease model.
- Roommate matching is offered.
- Applicants may request roommates, come as a group, or be matched.

## Student Eligibility
- Online students are accepted.
- The site states they must show proof of enrollment in at least one college class.
- The site also states classes must start within 30 days of lease start.

## Lease / Payment Operations
- Typical lease period is approximately 11.5 months.
- Lease agreement reflects the total rent amount divided into 12 equal installments.
- Site says they may offer additional/new lease terms.
- Rent is due on or before the 1st of each month.
- Grace period runs through the 5th at 11:59 PM.
- Late fee is 10% of the total rent amount.
- Autopay is available through the Resident Portal.

## Guarantor / Approval Model
- A guarantor is required for residents seeking to pay month by month, unless the full lease term is paid upfront.
- Guarantor requirement shown on site:
  - $65,000 annual income minimum
  - $110,000 combined if married
- Proof of income is required.
- Site says no credit check is run.
- Resident cannot be their own guarantor.
- Additional international guarantor / visa documentation rules are listed on the FAQ.

## Check-In / Move-In Process
- Site lists these pre-check-in items:
  - class schedule
  - student ID
  - move-in packet completion
  - balance and first month of rent paid
  - move-in slot selected

## Furnishing / Setup
- All units are fully furnished.
- Site emphasizes move-in convenience due to furnished apartments.

## Utility / Service Facts
### Confirmed across pages
- Residents are responsible for electricity.
- Internet is included in at least some wording on the site.

### Inconsistency warning
The original site uses inconsistent wording about included utilities/services.

Examples seen:
- Homepage/welcome page mentions sewage, valet trash, water, gas, and internet included.
- FAQ mentions internet, trash, water, and sewer included.
- Oasis page says one monthly payment includes rent, water, furniture, and internet.
- Cornerstone page says utilities include internet, water, sewer, and trash.

Conclusion:
- Electricity responsibility is safe.
- Exact included-utilities wording should be treated cautiously and confirmed before final polished copy.

## Parking / Packages / Amenities Access
- All communities offer free parking.
- One vehicle can be registered per resident.
- Parking information is emailed prior to move-in.
- Guest parking is first come, first served in non-numbered spaces.
- No commercial or RV parking.
- Packages can be delivered directly to apartments.
- Offices cannot accept packages.
- Amenities are property-specific and cannot be used across communities.

## Pet Policy
- Properties are pet free.
- ESA animals are allowed with proper paperwork.

## Internet Providers
- Cornerstone: Lumos
- Oasis: LUMOS
