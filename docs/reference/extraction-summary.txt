VUE Communities — organized extraction summary

Business / leasing model
- Student-only housing near Liberty University
- Two communities: Oasis and The VUE at Cornerstone
- Individual leases
- Roommate matching available
- Pricing shown per person / per room / per installment
- Typical lease period approximately 11.5 months
- Alternate lease terms may be available

Eligibility / application / guarantor
- Online students allowed with proof of enrollment in at least one college class
- Classes must start within 30 days of lease start
- Guarantor required for residents paying month-to-month unless full lease term is paid upfront
- Guarantor requirements shown on FAQ:
  - at least $65,000 annually
  - if married, $110,000 combined
  - proof of income required
  - no credit check stated
  - resident cannot be own guarantor
- Additional international guarantor / visa requirements listed on FAQ

Check-in / operations
- Pre-check-in items listed:
  - class schedule
  - student ID
  - move-in packet
  - pay balance and first month of rent
  - sign up for move-in slot
- Rent due on the 1st
- Five day grace period through the 5th at 11:59 PM
- Late fee stated as 10%
- Autopay available through resident portal
- Deposit refunds within 45 days after lease end

Utilities / furnishings / parking
- All units are furnished
- Residents are responsible for electricity
- Included-utilities wording varies by page on the live site
- All communities offer free parking
- One vehicle can be registered per resident
- Guest parking first-come first-served in non-numbered spaces
- Parking information emailed before move-in
- Packages can be sent directly to apartments; office cannot accept packages
