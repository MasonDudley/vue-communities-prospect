The Oasis

Amenities-first, right on the edge of Liberty.

Best for students who want the widest range of floor plans, stronger amenity access, and a community closely tied to campus movement.

  • Floor plans range from 1 to 6 bedrooms, including office/study layouts.
  • Clubhouse, study and game rooms, fitness center, pool, courtyards, and a nature trail.
  • Liberty Shuttle access adds another connection point for campus life.

Cornerstone

Streamlined 3BR/3BA living near restaurants and retail.

Best for students who want a private bathroom, walk-in closet, and a more focused residential setting off Greenview Drive.

  • Every apartment is a 3 bedroom, 3 bathroom layout.
  • Each resident has a private ensuite bathroom and walk-in closet.
  • Furnished units include a TV, washer/dryer, and gigabyte internet.

Parent and guarantor reassurance

Clear expectations, transparent installments, and a simpler lease structure.

VUE Communities highlights the details families usually want clarified before move-in: individual leases, furnished apartments, rent timing, and guarantor guidelines.

  • Installments are due on or before the 1st, with a grace period through the 5th at 11:59 PM.
  • A guarantor is required for month-to-month payment unless the lease term is paid in full upfront.
  • The guarantor income requirement shown is $65,000 annually or $110,000 combined if married.

FAQ highlights

Questions students ask first

Can online students live at VUE Communities?

Yes. Online students are welcome with proof of enrollment in at least one college class.

What utilities are included?

Electricity is billed separately. Internet is included, and the full utility bundle varies by community.

Are the apartments furnished?

Yes. Both communities are marketed as fully furnished.

Is parking available?

Both communities offer free parking, with one vehicle registration per resident.
