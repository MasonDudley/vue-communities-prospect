1BR and 2BR options

One-bedroom and two-bedroom options offer the smallest household sizes in the Oasis lineup, with 2BR pricing starting at $895.

  • 1BR availability varies; contact leasing
  • 2BR: $895
  • 2BR with office/study: $955

Office/study layouts

Oasis uses office/study plans to give smaller groups more privacy and additional academic workspace.

  • 3BR with office/study: $885

Lease structure

Individual leases and roommate matching come standard.

Students can list requested roommates or apply solo and be matched. Each resident leases their own room only, rather than taking responsibility for the apartment as a whole.
