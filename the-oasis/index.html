The Oasis at VUE Communities

Campus-edge living with more amenities and more floor plan options.

The Oasis is the amenity-forward community in the VUE Communities lineup. Located on the edge of Liberty's campus, it offers floor plans from 1 to 6 bedrooms plus Liberty Shuttle access.

Lease structure

Individual leases and roommate matching come standard.

Students can request roommates, apply with friends, or lease on their own and be matched. Each resident leases their own room rather than taking responsibility for the apartment as a whole.
