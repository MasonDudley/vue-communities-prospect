About VUE Communities

Student housing built around a simpler off-campus experience.

VUE Communities serves students in Lynchburg with two furnished apartment communities near Liberty University: The Oasis and The VUE at Cornerstone. Both communities are built around student-only leasing, individual leases, roommate matching, and a move-in process designed to be easier to manage.

The leasing office is centered at 40 Oasis Way, giving students and families one clear point of contact for tours, pricing questions, guarantor details, and move-in preparation.

What to expect

Clear leasing, furnished apartments, and options that fit different student priorities.

Student-only leasing

VUE Communities is positioned around college students, including online students with current enrollment documentation.

Furnished apartments

Both communities are marketed as fully furnished, helping students move in without bringing major furniture.

Two distinct communities

The Oasis emphasizes amenity access and floor plan variety, while Cornerstone focuses on a consistent 3BR/3BA layout with private bathrooms.

One brand, two living styles

Choose the setup that fits how you want to live during the school year.

The Oasis sits on the edge of Liberty's campus and offers the larger amenity package, plus floor plans from 1 to 6 bedrooms. Cornerstone offers a single 3BR/3BA layout in a residential neighborhood off Greenview Drive, with a private ensuite bathroom and walk-in closet for each resident.

  • The Oasis is the better fit for students who want more floor plan flexibility and stronger community amenities.
  • Cornerstone is the better fit for students who want consistent layout quality and more apartment-level privacy.
  • Both communities support roommate matching and individual leases.
Why students choose VUE

Strong fundamentals, backed by clear leasing information.

Individual leases

Residents lease their own room and are not responsible for a roommate's share of rent.

Roommate matching

Apply with a group, request roommates, or lease on your own and be matched.

Furnished units

Students move in with less to buy, borrow, or transport before the semester starts.

Transparent payment timing

Installments are due on the 1st, with a grace period through the 5th at 11:59 PM.

Guarantor guidance

Income requirements and payment rules are clearly laid out for families planning around the lease.

Community-specific amenities

Each property has its own amenity package, and amenities are not shared across communities.

Office support

Need help choosing between The Oasis and Cornerstone?

The leasing office can walk you through availability, pricing, roommate matching, guarantor requirements, and the move-in checklist for either community.
