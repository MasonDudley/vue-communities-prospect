Two distinct ways to live near Liberty University.

Vue Communities currently operates two student-only apartment options in Lynchburg: The Oasis and Cornerstone. Both are fully furnished, offer individual leases, and support roommate matching, but they serve different renter priorities.

The Oasis

More amenities. More variety. More campus energy.

Oasis is the flagship option for students who want broad layout flexibility and a stronger campus-adjacent feel. The live site highlights shuttle access, a clubhouse, study and game spaces, a fitness center, a resort-style pool, and a nature trail.

  • Floor plans from one bedroom to six bedrooms.
  • Current site notes access to the Liberty Shuttle Service.
  • Strong match for students who want convenience and amenity density.
Explore The Oasis
Cornerstone

Simple 3BR / 3BA living with private bathrooms for every resident.

Cornerstone is structured for students who know exactly what they want: a furnished three-bedroom apartment, a private ensuite bath, a walk-in closet, and proximity to local dining and retail.

  • Every resident gets a private bathroom and walk-in closet.
  • Furnished with a 50-inch TV, washer/dryer, and gigabyte internet.
  • Strong fit for friend groups or students who want a more predictable layout.
Explore Cornerstone
Both communities are student-only

Online students are also accepted with current enrollment documentation, according to the FAQ.

Both communities are furnished

Students do not need to bring large furniture or rent a moving truck.

Both communities use individual leases

You rent your room only, which reduces roommate-related financial exposure.

Both communities offer free parking

The live FAQ says parking is first come, first served, with one vehicle per resident.
