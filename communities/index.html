Two distinct ways to live near Liberty University.

VUE Communities offers two student-only apartment options in Lynchburg: The Oasis and The VUE at Cornerstone. Both communities are furnished, use individual leases, and support roommate matching, but they serve different living priorities.

The Oasis

More amenities, more variety, and more campus energy.

The Oasis is the best fit for students who want broad layout flexibility and stronger community amenities, from a pool and fitness center to study space and outdoor gathering areas.

  • Floor plans from one bedroom to six bedrooms.
  • Clubhouse, study and game rooms, courtyards, grilling areas, and a nature trail.
  • Liberty Shuttle access.

Cornerstone

Simple 3BR / 3BA living with a private bathroom for every resident.

Cornerstone is built for students who know exactly what they want: a furnished three-bedroom apartment, a walk-in closet, in-unit laundry, and more apartment-level privacy.

  • Every resident gets a private ensuite bathroom and walk-in closet.
  • Furnished with a 50-inch TV, washer/dryer, and gigabyte internet.
  • A strong fit for groups of friends or students who want one predictable layout.

Both communities are student-only

Online students are also accepted with current enrollment documentation.

Both communities are furnished

Students do not need to bring large furniture or build out an apartment from scratch.

Both communities use individual leases

Each resident leases their room only, which reduces roommate-related financial exposure.

Both communities offer free parking

Parking is first come, first served, with one vehicle allowed per resident.
