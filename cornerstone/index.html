How pricing works

Installments, lease structure, and what is included.

Installment structure

Lease totals are divided into 12 equal installments even though the lease term is typically about 11.5 months.

Individual lease protection

Your pricing is tied to your room only, so you are not responsible for another roommate's balance.

Utility structure

Electricity is resident-paid. Internet is included, and the rest of the bundled utilities vary by community.
