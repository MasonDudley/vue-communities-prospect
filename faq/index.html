Frequently asked questions

Clear answers for students, parents, and guarantors.

These questions cover the lease details, policies, and move-in topics students, parents, and guarantors most often ask about for The Oasis and The VUE at Cornerstone.

Leasing basics

Application, lease, and payment

Can online students live at VUE Communities?

Yes. Online students are welcome with proof of enrollment in at least one college class, and classes must start within 30 days of the lease start date.

What is a rent installment?

The total lease amount is divided into 12 equal installments, even though the lease term is typically about 11.5 months.

When is rent due?

Installments are due on or before the 1st of each month, with a grace period through the 5th at 11:59 PM.

Can I pay through the Resident Portal?

Yes. Autopay is available through the Resident Portal.

Parents and guarantors

Protection and qualification

Why do I need a guarantor?

A guarantor is required for residents paying month to month. No guarantor is needed if the total lease amount is paid upfront.

What are the guarantor income requirements?

The listed income requirement is $65,000 annually for one guarantor or $110,000 combined if married, with proof of income required.

Is a credit check required?

The FAQ states that no credit check is run.

What are the benefits of an individual lease?

You are responsible for your own room and charges only, not for a roommate's unpaid share of the apartment.

Apartment details

Furnishings, utilities, and bed sizes

Are the units furnished?

Yes. Both communities are marketed as fully furnished.

What utilities are included?

Electricity is billed separately. Internet is included, and leasing can confirm the full utility bundle for your community.

What bed size should I plan for?

Cornerstone, 1BR Oasis, and 2BR Oasis use Full XL beds. Oasis 2BR with office, 3BR with office, 4BR, and 6BR layouts use Twin XL beds.

Can residents use amenities at another community?

No. Amenities are reserved for residents of their own property.

Move-in and daily life

Parking, providers, transfers, and pets

What do I need before move-in?

Residents are asked to submit a class schedule, student ID, and move-in packet, pay their balance and first month of rent, and sign up for a move-in slot.

Where do residents and guests park?

Parking is first come, first served, with one vehicle allowed per resident. Guest parking is also first come, first served in non-numbered spaces.

What internet provider serves the communities?

The FAQ lists Lumos for Cornerstone and LUMOS for The Oasis.

Can I transfer rooms or communities?

Room transfers can be requested in writing if another room is available. Community transfers can be applied for, but not until the current lease term is over.

What is the pet policy?

The properties are pet-free, but ESA animals are allowed with proper paperwork.
