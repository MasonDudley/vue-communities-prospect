How to read the pricing

Installments, roommate structure, and value positioning.

Installment structure

The FAQ says lease totals are divided into 12 equal installments even though the lease term is about 11.5 months.

Individual lease protection

Your pricing is tied to your room only. You are not responsible for other roommates' balances.

Utility details vary by community

Published utility inclusions differ by property, while electricity remains resident-paid. Contact leasing for the latest details.

Best-fit guidance

Which plan type makes the most sense?

  • Oasis 4BR / 6BR: best for price-sensitive students who still want a furnished, amenity-rich community.
  • Oasis office/study layouts: best for students who want quieter academic space and fewer roommates.
  • Cornerstone 3BR / 3BA: best for students who want a private bathroom and consistent layout quality.
