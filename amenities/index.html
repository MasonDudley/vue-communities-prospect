Saltwater pool

A signature community feature for relaxing, socializing, and warm-weather downtime.

24-hour fitness center

The Oasis FAQ references around-the-clock fitness access.

Clubhouse

Shared community space for casual meetups and resident events.

Study and game rooms

Dedicated spaces for focus time and social time within the same community.

Courtyards and grilling areas

Outdoor gathering space adds more flexibility beyond the apartment itself.

Nature trail and shuttle access

Amenities at Cornerstone

Smaller community footprint, stronger apartment-level privacy.

Private ensuite bathrooms

Every resident has a bathroom connected to their own bedroom.

Walk-in closets

Each bedroom includes better storage and a more complete personal setup.

Outdoor seating and grilling

Shared exterior gathering space without a large amenity complex.

Gigabyte internet

Cornerstone includes gigabyte internet service.

Washer and dryer

50-inch TV

Fully furnished units

Roommate matching

Free parking
